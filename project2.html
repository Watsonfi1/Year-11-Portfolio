End Users

This project is targeted towards someone who wants to play Tic Tac Toe and has time to do so. The best group of people to target with a Tic Tac Toe game is kids, this is due to them having lots of spare time, friends to play with, and they want to play easy and quick games. We will make instructions to explain the game to kids who would want to play it and make sure they would be able to understand it. For replayability we will add a score system and changing who starts so they can play multiple times with friends easily.

Usability

Usability of the Tic Tac Toe game will make it easy to understand the rules and how to make a move. We will make sure that its easy to select different modes and difficulties.

I addressed this by:

  • I will make sure that the final product has an easy way to tell if your playing multiplayer or singleplayer and easy to tell other things we add. It should work well whilst still being really easy to understand.
  • I will also make the way that you make moves quick and easy. I will try to stop you from having to type a lot to make a move for easyness and usability.
